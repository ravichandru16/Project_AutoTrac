About Voltrac CRM

Voltrac CRM is a polished automotive sales management platform built to help dealerships and sales teams manage leads, opportunities, test drives, activities, and analytics in one sleek interface.

  • Branded dashboard with pie-chart analytics and Voltrac logo-driven transitions
  • Live app data reflected in lead source, performance, and completion metrics
  • Responsive CRM modules for leads, contacts, accounts, and activities
  • Dark theme styling with premium visual polish
